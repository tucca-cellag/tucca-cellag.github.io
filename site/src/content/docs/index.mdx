## Our projects

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="tucca-rna-seq" icon="seti:python">
    A fast, automated, reproducible RNA-Seq workflow built on Snakemake v8+ —
    from quality control through differential expression and functional
    enrichment, with Tufts HPC guidance.

    [Read the docs →](/tucca-rna-seq/introduction/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Computational blue-melanin design" icon="seti:pipeline">
    De novo design of stable blue (and purple/green) melanin pigments via
    RFdiffusion motif scaffolding of Y-containing pentapeptides — deep-learning
    protein design applied to sustainable colorants.

    [About the project →](/projects/blue-melanin/)
  </Card>
  <Card title="CAAIL — Cellular Agriculture AI Library" icon="open-book">
    A curated, openly-licensed library at the intersection of cellular
    agriculture and AI: peer-reviewed papers, software, databases, and
    per-species datasets.

    [Open the library ↗](https://tucca-cellag.github.io/caail/)
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
